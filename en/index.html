About us

Observer Studio is a studio first founded by a group of passionate maker enthusiasts (students) in 2021. We are focused on developing and innovating in the field of computer technology. Our team possess diverse backgrounds and rich experience, and the passion for technology drives us to continuously pursue excellence. From the very beginning, we have focused on researching and developing a variety of technological projects, including game design and software development. In our projects, we integrate creativity and technology, striving to break through traditional boundaries and create products with unique value. Our goal is not only to solve practical problems but also to inspire innovative thinking and promote technological advancement. At Observer Studio, we believe that technology is not just a tool, but a platform for realizing dreams. Our studio is not only a laboratory for technological research and development, but also a community filled with creativity and a spirit of collaboration. Welcome to join us, as we explore the endless possibilities of computer technology and work together to create a brighter future!
