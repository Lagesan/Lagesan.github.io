About Schoolent

Schoolent is a school community founded in a Jiangsu Kunshan senior school, serving as a platform for internal students to connect, collaborate, and grow together at the beginning.

Founded by students for students, we understand the unique challenges schools and the students face in today's digital age. Our mission is to empower institutions to deliver exceptional education while fostering meaningful connections within their community.
